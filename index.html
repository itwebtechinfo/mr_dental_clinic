Dr. Ekhlaq Ahmed is a highly experienced dental professional with more than 9 years of hands-on clinical experience in modern and regenerative dentistry. He earned his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (B.D.S) degree from Jamia Millia Islamia, a Central Government University , which is well known for its strong academic standards.

After completing his education, Dr. Ekhlaq Ahmed worked in multiple well-known hospitals and dental clinics , where he gained wide experience in different dental treatments. His work includes everything from basic to advanced & aesthetic dental procedures.

His core belief is based on Regenerative Dentistry, an approach that aims to preserve natural teeth, promote natural healing, and ensure long-term oral health.

Dr. Ekhlaq Ahmed regularly upgrades his skills and knowledge and holds respected international certifications:

dti Dental Tribune International New York, USA International Centre of Dental Excellence
Advanced Programs • Global Dental Standards

Dr. Ekhlaq Ahmed also supports responsible healthcare practices and is actively involved in the Force Against AMR (Antimicrobial Resistance) awareness program by Cipla , with support from THE TIMES OF INDIA . This reflects his commitment to safe antibiotic use and preventive dental care.
